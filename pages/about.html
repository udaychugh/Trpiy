what is tripy ?

All of us love to travel and explore new places with our loved ones specially with our friends. But after the trip there comes the boring task of calculating expenses and splitting up which is by the way a drag. Hi, my name is TRIPY and I am here to make your life a little less boring by helping you in this task.

how to use tripy

I want to be your friend so I’ll tell you about myself and how I can be of use to you.

Share the total expense of the trip along with number of days you stayed (which is obviously fixed).

One by one introduce me to everyone.

Tell me your name , budget for the trip and the number of days one stayed for the trip.

I’ll do the boring task for you and tell you the individual amount ought to be paid by everyone.

Anyone can download the receipt through me (an excel file).

You can pay your amount via your cards.

Now we are friends forever. The green paid sign is the symbol of our true friendship .
